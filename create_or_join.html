Elegant real-time note-taking

Start a meeting as the note-taker

Take-note is an elegant and simple way to take notes, share them in real-time and allow multiple participants to interact with the flow of data as fluidly as the conversation flows. Every keystroke that you enter will be rendered as quickly as you change your notes. Any changes to the notes will be hilighted so participants can see where the conversation is currently active. As well, any top-level sections that are discussed can be easily collapsed allowing viewers to choose what the focus of their experience is.

1. Click "Start"
2. Start typing
3. Share your work by sharing your link
